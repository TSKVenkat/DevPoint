Guidelines

  • Keep It Real (and Respectful): Engage with others like you would on your best day of clean code. Respect all perspectives, no flame wars here!
  • Stay On Topic: Love cats? So do we, but let's keep project and profile spaces focused on tech talk (or at least tech-adjacent cat talk).
  • Constructive Criticism Only: If you're reviewing code or giving feedback, make it helpful. Channel your inner debugger, not your inner troll.
  • Help a Fellow Dev Out: Stack Overflow vibes here; share resources, solutions, and spare a line or two of encouragement.
  • Share, Don’t Spam: Links and resources are welcome, but no one wants a feed full of ads. Keep it genuine and relevant.
  • File Sharing? Keep It Safe: When sharing files, double-check they’re clean, safe, and don’t trigger any alarms. No surprises here!
  • Be Mindful of Bandwidth: Got an amazing project screenshot? Great! But keep image sizes optimized—no one loves loading times longer than code compile.
  • Celebrate the Wins : Got a new feature? Debugged a nightmare bug? Share it! We’re here for the confetti, even if it's virtual.
  • Flag When Necessary: See something off? Use the flag feature to keep our space positive and safe.
  • Account Responsibility: You logged in through your own Google account, which means you’re responsible for your account’s security. I’m not the one resetting your passwords—so keep it safe!
  • Have Fun, Code Hard: Remember, we’re all here to learn, grow, and have a blast. Happy coding!
